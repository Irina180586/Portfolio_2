Irina Gadimova

Hi there, thanks for stopping by! I’m a User Researcher and a UX/UI Designer who creates human-centric, user-friendly and engaging products.

I'm a person who loves to explore and experience new things. With that in mind, after graduating from my home country's university, I moved to China and worked in international logistics for five years. While building a career in the transportation and supply chain industries, I have always been curious about culinary arts and imagined myself as part of a talented chef's team. Even though that always seemed beyond the reach, my plan worked out, and my dream came true. I finished culinary school in Canada and worked in restaurants for four years.

Being empathic by nature, my professional background, curiosity, and determination to hear and understand the client/guest brought me to learning and experiencing human-centric design.

Organizing cargo delivery in compliance with all needs and expectations of clients, combined with the experience in the hospitality industry where we create an unforgettable dining experience to have our patrons come back, turned up as assets to my UX/UI design journey. Now I'm confident in solving user's problems and creating an intricate design of customers' wants and businesses' musts'.

I am always open to new projects, so don't be shy - let's chat.
